Tulips

Tulips (Tulipa) form a genus of spring-bloomin perennial herbaceous bukbiferous gepphytes (having bulbs as storage organs).The flowers are usually large, showy and brightly colored, generally red, pink, yellow, or white (usually In warm colors).

Liliaceae Tulip

Liliaceae Tulip is a membr of the lily family,Liliaceae,along with 14 other genera,where it is most closely related to Amana, Erythronium and Gagea in the tribe Lilieae.There are about 75 species,and these are divided among four subgenera.The name "tulip" is thought to be derived from a Persian word for turban,which it may have been thought to resemble.Tulips originally were found in a band strectching from Southern Europe to Central Asia,but since the seventeenth century have become widely naturalised and cultivated.
